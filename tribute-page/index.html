About Van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter who largely remained unknown while he was alive (source: Wikipedia).

Despite his struggles with his mental health, van Gogh found beauty in many things, and this is evident in his paintings.

    Here is a chronology of some of van Gogh's works:

  • 1853 Vincent van Gogh is born
  • 1881 "Road with Pollard Willows and Man with Broom", Drawing, Pencil, washed
  • 1885 "Still life with Potatoes", Painting, Oil on Canvas
  • 1888 "The Harvest", Watercolor, Black Chalk and Gouache on pink ingres paper
  • 1889 "Wheat Field with Cypresses", Painting, Oil on Canvas
  • 1889 "The Olive Trees", Painting, Oil on Canvas
  • 1889 "The Starry Night", Painting, Oil on Canvas
  • 1890 "The Church at Auvers", Painting, Oil on Canvas
  • 1890 "Road with Cypress and Star", Painting, Oil on Canvas
  • 1890"Wheatfield with Crows" -believed to be van Gogh's last seen painting
  • 1890 van Gogh is fatally shot - officials are unable to determine if it is self-inflicted.

  • Based on the chronology provided at the van Gogh Gallery.
